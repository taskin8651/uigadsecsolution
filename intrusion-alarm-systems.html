Intrusion Alarm Solution

Detect Unauthorized Entry Before It Becomes a Security Risk

Gadsec Solutions provides intrusion alarm systems that help detect unauthorized entry, movement, forced door opening and perimeter breach attempts across commercial, industrial, institutional and residential sites.

Our solutions include motion sensors, magnetic door contacts, alarm panels, hooters, sirens, keypads, perimeter detection devices and integration with CCTV or monitoring systems.

  • Motion sensors, door contacts and glass break detection
  • Alarm control panels, keypads, sirens and hooters
  • Perimeter detection for boundary and restricted zones
  • CCTV, access control and remote alert integration support
