About Gadsec Solutions

Trusted security, safety, and surveillance solutions provider since 1998.

Company Overview

Reliable Security, Fire Safety & Integrated Infrastructure Solutions

Gadsec Solutions is a professional security and safety solutions provider serving commercial, industrial, institutional and government clients. Since 1998, the company has focused on delivering dependable systems backed by consultation, installation, testing, commissioning and support.

Our approach is simple: understand the site requirement, recommend the right solution, execute clean installation, test every system properly, and provide long-term service support.

  • CCTV surveillance, fire detection, access control and intrusion alarm solutions
  • Consultation, site survey, supply, installation and commissioning
  • AMC, troubleshooting, maintenance and technical support
  • Solutions for commercial, industrial, institutional and government projects
