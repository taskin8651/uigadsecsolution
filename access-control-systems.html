Access Control Solution

Secure Entry Management for Offices, Industries & Institutions

Gadsec Solutions provides access control systems that help organizations manage who can enter specific areas, when they can enter, and how access activity is recorded.

Our access control solutions include biometric devices, RFID readers, smart card access, door controllers, EM locks, attendance integration and multi-location access management.

  • Biometric, RFID and smart card access systems
  • Door controllers, EM locks and exit switches
  • Attendance and reporting integration
  • Multi-door and multi-location access control support

02
