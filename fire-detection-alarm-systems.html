Our Process

Fire Alarm Project Execution Process

We follow a structured process from site assessment to installation, testing, commissioning and support.

01
Site Survey

We review building layout, floor areas, risk zones, detector locations and emergency alert requirements.

02
System Design

03
Installation

04
